Day Two

Legend of the Purple Hairpin（紫钗记）

Kicking off day two's festival is an opening of live Cantonese opera. It is the famous play of "The Legend of the Purple Hairpin"(紫钗记) starring the famous Chor Fung Ming Cantonese Opera Troupe.

In the Mood for Love （花样年华）

Following the live stage opera comes with a film screening of "In the Mood for Love” （花样年华）. Critically acclaimed and considered as one of the best Hong Kong films of all time. Set in 1960s Hong Kong, it follows a man Chow Mo-wan (Tony Leung) and Su Li-zhen (Maggie Cheung) who slowly fall in love after finding out their spouses are having affairs.

Basic conversational cantonese lesson

Come join us with this fun and interactive lesson of Cantonese led by CCSF's Cantonese professsor Grace Yu.

Day Three

Community Street Fair

Kicking off the last day of the festival is a community street fair along Grant Avenue. Have a walkthrough of different booths introducing you to different topics about cantonese history, food, landmarks, culture, and more.

Calligraphy

After the streefair have a small lesson on Chinese calligraphy.

Closing Ceremony

Closing off our festival is a closing parade of lion dance and dragon dance meeting back on the main stage on Grant Avenue.

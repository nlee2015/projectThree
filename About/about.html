Cantonese Rising is a three day festival which promotes and celebrates the Cantonese language and culture in the heart of San Francisco's Chinatown, the city's hub of Chinese culture. With the push for greater usage of Mandarin by the Chinese government, fears have risen that Cantonese might be a forgotten language in the future. Our mission is to advocate for the language and culture through fun and interactive actvities that can be enjoyed by everyone not just Cantonese speakers but everyone.

Our Partners

Our partners includes Save Cantonese and the Cantonese Alliance, two movements deciated in promoting Cantonese language and culture. Both of these organizations promotes Cantonese language through the preservation and significance of Cantonese language programs and the significance of preserving these programs for future generations.
